All About Turtles

Turtles are amazing animals. Turtles are small, slow, and cute! I think turtles are great because you can keep them in your own little aquarium! There are many different types of turtles. Many people confuse turtles for tortoises, the difference is that tortoises live on land, but turtles live in water. Turtles only come on land when birth and when laying eggs. I love turtles.
